Our Projects

From carbon market analytics to policy frameworks, our projects address critical gaps in climate finance and environmental governance.

Platform

Carbon Arbitrage App

Global carbon price analytics platform identifying arbitrage opportunities and market inefficiencies across international carbon markets.

Framework

Decarbonization Policy

Trade-off optimization framework for governments balancing economic growth, social equity, and emissions reduction in resource-constrained environments.

Dashboard

Landfill Emissions

Interactive GHRP methane emissions dashboard providing real-time data visualization for waste management policy and climate action planning.

Policy Paper

Climate Finance Reimagined

Novel financing framework restructuring how international climate funds flow to LMICs, emphasizing adaptation capacity and local ownership.

Research

Climate Tech U.S. Networks

Study with NetZero Lab TU Munich mapping innovation clusters and technology transfer pathways for emerging market adoption.

Coming Soon Partnership

UN Climate Initiative

Major collaboration with UN development agencies to pilot Envira frameworks in Sub-Saharan African nations.

Framework

Sustainable Budget Strategy

A data-driven fiscal framework enabling smarter long-term planning by quantifying trade-offs across tax policy, fund balance use, and major infrastructure or green-investment decisions.

Dashboard

Municipal Scenario Simulator

Dynamic visualization system modeling 5–20 year impacts of Town Hall relocation, pool upgrades, tax-levy changes, and energy-efficiency initiatives to guide Board decision-making.

Policy Analysis

Future-Ready North Castle

Strategic roadmap aligning municipal finance with environmental resilience, revealing how sustainable investments—solar, EV fleets, building upgrades—strengthen long-term fiscal health.
